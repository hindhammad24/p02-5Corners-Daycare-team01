About Us

At 5 corners daycare, we provide a secure supportive and trusting environment in which children can develop and grow at their own pace. We offer creative and stimulating child-centered activities that encourage social, emotional, intellectual and physical growth and well being.

The goals of our program are to nurture self-confidence, self-discipline, cooperation and social skills. We respect and acknowledge color, culture, age, gender, ability, race, appearance and social-economic status.

We offer infant, toddler, and preschool care with both full-time and part-time spots available. We offer a variety of indoor and outdoor activities that support healthy development.
